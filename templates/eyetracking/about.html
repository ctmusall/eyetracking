About Us


We are the eyeTracking team and we have developed a camera that will detect your eyes while driving and alert
the driver if his/her pupil is not detected for a short amount of time or if gazing away
from the road for too long.

How Are We Different?

We provide detailed analytics that are tracked through the eyeTracker that records speed, location, and where
you are looking. These analytics can be viewed on our website or on smartphone app and can also be compared
to other drivers. These analytics are feedback for the individual driver and/or business
to improve for the future to create more safe driving habits.
