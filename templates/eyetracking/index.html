Hello!

Welcome to the eyeTracking project. The eyeTracker is designed to track your eyes while you are driving to improve driving habits.
Not only will the eyeTracker alert the driver when not detecting the pupils, but the eyeTracker also provides detailed analytics for the driver
or company to view their statistics and to compare with others around the world.

Getting Started:

The eyeTracker uses a Raspberry Pi which is a small computer that has a camera attached
to keep track of the eyes so to get started using the Raspberry Pi, just follow these simple steps:

  1. Place the eyeTracker at the front dash of your car where the eyes can be easily seen with the attached camera.
  2. Download the eyeTracker app on your smartphone and connect to the eyeTracker via Bluetooth.
  3. All that is left is to make an account on the app and you are ready to go! Stay safe out there eyeTrackers!
